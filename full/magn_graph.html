MAGNITUDE HISTOGRAM WITH KERNEL DENSITY ESTIMATION

The above histogram depicts the distribution of MAGNITUDE based on date mentioned in the dataset. This histogram is accompanied with Kernel Density Estimation(KDE). The magnitudes given in the dataset range from 5.5 to 9.1 .
Kernel density estimation or KDE is a non-parametric way to estimate the probability density function of a random variable. In other words the aim of KDE is to find probability density function (PDF) for a given dataset. KDE gives the actual shape of the curve.
