TYPES OF EARTHQUAKE

The above plot depicts the type of Earthquake (or the reason of the cause of earthquake). This countplot depicts the type of earthquake,whether it was an actual quake (i.e caused due to movement in the Earth's crust), Nuclear Explosion, Explosion or Rock Burst. All these are the causes which actually caused an Earthquake to occur.
Conclusion : Most of the quakes in the dataset were the effect of an actual earthquake. A very few percent of quakes were caused due to Nuclear Explosions.
